# Privacy Policy

Last updated: [2025-12-15]

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how **Fukuoka Shopping Guide** (“the App”) handles user information.
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

The App is designed to provide shopping and travel guidance for Hakata and Tenjin areas in Fukuoka, Japan.

---

## 2. Information We Do Not Collect

The App does **not** collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

Specifically, we do **not** collect:
- Name
- Email address
- Phone number
- Account information
- Photos, contacts, or files
- Precise location data
- Payment or financial information

---

## 3. Automatically Collected Information

The App itself does **not** collect analytics, tracking data, or usage logs.

If the App uses Apple-provided services (such as App Store distribution),
Apple may collect limited, anonymized data according to their own privacy policy.
We do not have access to this data.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

The App does **not** integrate third-party SDKs that collect personal data
(such as advertising networks, analytics tools, or social login services).

If external links (e.g., maps or websites) are accessed, those services are governed
by their own privacy policies.

---

## 5. Data Storage

- No user data is stored on external servers.
- All content is provided locally within the App.
- No cloud storage or account system is used.

---

## 6. Children’s Privacy

The App does not target children specifically and does not knowingly collect
any personal information from children under the age of 13.
